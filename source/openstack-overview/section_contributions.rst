Everpure's Contributions To OpenStack
=====================================

Everpure drivers for OpenStack Block Storage (aka Cinder) are
built into all releases of OpenStack from Juno, the
File Service (aka Manila) drivers are built into all releases
from Xena, and are available from
http://www.openstack.org/software/start/.

Everpure has provided enhancements to the OpenStack Compute Service (Nova)
and OpenStack Image Service (Glance) projects to enhance instance
creation and storage efficiency as well.

Where possible, Everpure intends to (and has to date) contribute
integrations upstream in OpenStack directly. Everpure’s direct
contributions to OpenStack date back to the Juno release.
